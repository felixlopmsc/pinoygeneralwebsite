# Pinoy General Insurance Services - Homepage PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a complete WordPress-style homepage for Pinoy General Insurance Services, a local independent insurance agency in Cerritos, CA. Modern, professional, conversion-focused design.

## User Personas
- **Homeowners** seeking home insurance in Southern California
- **Drivers** looking for affordable auto insurance
- **Renters** needing renter's coverage
- **Business owners** requiring commercial insurance

## Core Requirements
- Sticky header with logo, navigation (Home, Insurance Tips, Quote Center), Get Quote CTA
- Hero section with insurance document background, headline, CTAs
- "Best of Business Rate 2025" awards section with stats (2000+ clients, 30+ years)
- Services grid: Home, Auto, Renter's, Commercial Insurance
- Google Reviews testimonials (Martin Keith Aaron Lao, Andy Sevilla)
- Contact section with static form, phone, email, social links
- Fully responsive (mobile-first, hamburger menu)
- All "Get Quote" buttons scroll to contact section
